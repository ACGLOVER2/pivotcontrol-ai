# Product Roadmap — PivotControl AI

*Beta → Productized → SaaS*

---

## Current State (Beta — April 2026)

PivotControl AI is in active beta. The core product is a manual audit service: a seller submits a call transcript plus a completed intake form, and receives a structured Pivot Control Audit Report within 48 hours.

**What exists today:**
- Pivot Control Index™ (PCI) — five-metric scoring framework
- Role-specific rubrics — AD (SC-led and AD-led), SC, Enterprise AE, Founder
- Intake form system — three persona-specific forms (AD, SC, AE/Founder)
- Audit report template — eight-section DOCX format
- Context-first scoring process — intake required before any transcript is scored
- CONFIRMED / NOT APPLICABLE finding labels

**Beta metrics:**
- 3 audits completed (Antoine Wade / GDIT, Kamau Miller / ForgeAI, Kyle Walsh / GDIT)
- 3 distinct call types covered (SC-led demo, Founder peer intro, SC calibration)
- PCI range observed: 47 (Developing) to 88 (Executive)
- Primary product insight: intake context is the difference between accurate coaching and pattern-matching

---

## Phase 1 — Paid Beta (Current → 30 Days)

**Goal:** First paying client at $497. Proof that the audit delivers value worth paying for.

**Offer:** 30-Day Pivot Control Audit  
- 1 recorded call (Teams, Zoom, or transcript)  
- Completed intake form  
- Full PivotControl AI audit report  
- 45-minute walkthrough session  
- 30-Day development plan

**Target client:** Enterprise AE or Account Director closing $500K–$5M deals in tech or logistics who has felt their authority erode under rapid-fire executive questioning.

**Go-to-market:** Direct outreach via LinkedIn DM using the beta research framing. 10 DMs sent in batches of 3 (Day 1, 3, 5). Offer 2 free analyses for feedback, then convert to paid.

---

## Phase 2 — Productized Service (30–90 Days)

**Goal:** 5–10 paying clients. Repeatable delivery. Case studies documented.

**Offer evolution:**
- Individual audit: $497–$997
- 3-audit package (before/during/after coaching): $1,200–$2,500
- Team pilot (3–5 reps, 30 days): $3,000–$5,000

**Intake system:** Formalize the intake form as a standard submission process. Clients complete the intake digitally before submitting any transcript. No intake = no audit.

**Account Intelligence layer:** Build out the account history fields in the intake to enable fully account-specific language coaching. This is the product's primary differentiator from generic AI communication tools.

**Rubric expansion:**
- Complete the Founder/AE peer positioning rubric
- Add the SC diagnostic pivot curriculum
- Build the rapid-fire resilience scoring into a standalone module

---

## Phase 3 — SaaS Platform (90+ Days)

**Goal:** Automate the scoring layer. Build a subscription product.

**Core SaaS features:**
- Call transcript ingestion (Teams, Zoom, Gong, Chorus export)
- Automated PCI scoring against role-specific rubrics
- Account intelligence database (intake history per account, per seller)
- Language coaching engine — account-specific reframes, not templates
- 30-day development plan generation
- Progress tracking across audit cycles

**Subscription model:**
- Individual: $49–$99/month
- Team dashboard: $399–$999/month  
- Enterprise: Custom pricing

**The moat:** The account intelligence layer. Other tools analyze transcripts. PivotControl AI scores calls against the objective they were designed to achieve, using account-specific context to produce language coaching that is actually usable in the next call.

## The Positioning

PivotControl AI is not positioned as:
- A communication coaching app
- A filler word counter
- A generic presentation training tool

It is positioned as:

> **Revenue Communication Intelligence for enterprise sellers** — specifically measuring and improving authority under rapid-fire executive questioning in high-stakes deal conversations.

The problem it solves is not a soft skill problem. It is a deal velocity problem. Enterprise AEs don't lose deals because of bad strategy. They lose momentum during unstructured pivots — and momentum is revenue.
